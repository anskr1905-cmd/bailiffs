Frequently Asked Questions

Is your advice really free?

Yes, our initial consultation and advice are completely free. We only charge fees if you decide to proceed with a formal debt solution that requires our ongoing services.

How quickly can you help with bailiff action?

For urgent bailiff situations, we can often provide immediate advice over the phone. If you need representation, we can usually contact bailiffs within 24 hours.

Do you handle cases nationwide?

Yes, we provide advice and assistance for bailiff action across England and Wales.

What information should I have ready when I contact you?

If possible, have your debt details, any letters from bailiffs or courts, and information about your financial situation ready. However, don't delay contacting us if you don't have all this information.
