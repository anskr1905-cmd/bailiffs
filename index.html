How We Can Help You

Know Your Rights

Bailiffs have strict rules they must follow. We'll explain what bailiffs can and cannot do when they visit your home or business, and help you understand your legal protections.

Stop Bailiff Action

Learn effective strategies to stop bailiffs from taking your goods, including negotiation techniques, payment arrangements, and legal procedures that can halt enforcement action.

Debt Solutions

Explore various debt solutions including payment plans, debt relief orders, individual voluntary arrangements (IVAs), and bankruptcy options tailored to your situation.

Challenge Incorrect Debts

If you believe a debt is incorrect or the bailiff has made an error, we can guide you through the process of challenging the debt and disputing bailiff fees.
